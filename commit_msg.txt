feat(apotheosis): eight-kit pipeline, full SDF body, 23 post-processing techniques

Eight-kit rendering pipeline:
- BoneKit: 26-bone skeleton with per-bone weight, aspect ratio, axis range
- ModelKit: piecewise-cosine radius_at() profiles with joint continuity enforcement
- MaterialKit: six material tags (Skin/Hair/Jacket/Boot/Metal/Eye) + Schlick Fresnel
- LightingKit: key + 3 fills + rim + hemisphere ambient + ACES filmic tonemap
- ClothingKit: radial garment push, seam darkening, contact shadow at skin/fabric edge
- HairKit: 500-strand curtain-cut, five scalp zones, Kajiya-Kay anisotropic specular
- PhysicsKit: per-particle inertial lag cache with per-material smoothing weights
- RenderKit: DoF jitter, n_copies scatter, alpha/emission scaling per copy

SDF body geometry (replaces bone-capsule system):
- sdf_torso: superellipsoid cross-section with plerp ax/az profiles along Y
- sdf_arm_r, sdf_forearm_r, sdf_leg_r: tapered elliptic capsules per limb segment
- sdf_body: consolidated smooth union of all limbs
- smin (Inigo Quilez polynomial): smooth minimum joint blending at all joints
- Analytical normals from SDF gradient — no finite differences, no faceting
- SDF AO: IQ 5-step geometric-decay normal-march, exact to SDF precision
- SDF thickness probe: inward -n march for subsurface scattering from SDF thickness
- Importance sampling: particles placed on surface shell, ~100% acceptance rate
